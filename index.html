About Me

Trained as a general and industrial engineer, I always felt a particular attraction to numbers and data in general. I find the objectivity that analytical summaries bring to be reassuring and a strong basis on which to build decisions or to use when arguing or fact-checking. I also believe that with the right tools and presentation, data can convey stories in a very compelling and insightful way through the art of data visualization. By building dashboards or just interactive visuals, it is also possible to engage and involve users. The world of data is vast and from the simplest bar charts to the complex details of data engineering and machine learning, the possibilities, opportunities and roles are endless.
I personally want to focus on analytics and data science, from creating and gathering data to the restitution of insights through reports and dashboards, while going through data cleaning, querying, modelling and building visualizations.
